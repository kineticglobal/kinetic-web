Where We Work

Kinetic Global has chapters on college campuses all across the country. At each of these schools, Kinetic finds passionate student leaders who are interested in transforming their theoretical conversations about pressing social issues into tangible action. Kinetic provides the resources and structure to empower these students to affect change. These students then form teams to begin impacting change.
