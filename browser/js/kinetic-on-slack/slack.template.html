Kinetic On Slack

Join 200+ student social innovators and advisors across the US to develop projects making real change in your community.

Why should my Kinetic team join slack?

  1. Directly access Kinetic members and advisors across the US. Do you have a burning questions about your issue? Our slack account has channels set up for each campus, for each issue, and for other assorted helpful topics.
  2. Easy to organize your team’s resources in a private slack channel. As your team develops your project, you might gain new team members. Using slack will allow you to search old messages in one location.
  3. Work on your Kinetic project on your computer application, online, or on mobile.

More Resources:

  1. Slack’s Help Center is a great place to start with questions.
  2. Once you join kineticglobal.slack.com, the #slackhelp channel is for slack support.
  3. Download Slack for your devices and check out your notification settings after joining slack.
